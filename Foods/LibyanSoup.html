Description

Libyan soup (Shorba Libiya) is a warm, spiced tomato-based soup made with lamb or beef, chickpeas or lentils, and small pasta like orzo or vermicelli. It‚Äôs flavored with fragrant spices like turmeric, cinnamon, and cayenne, and often finished with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon. Ingredients:

  • 300g lamb or beef (cubed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 fresh tomato (grated or finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas or red lentils
  • 1 stalk celery (optional, chopped)
  • 1/4 cup small pasta (orzo/vermicelli/rice)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1.5 liters water or broth
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro (chopped, for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

  1. Saut√©: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add Meat: Add the cubed lamb or beef. Brown the meat on all sides.
  3. Add Tomato: Stir in the tomato paste and grated tomato. Let it cook for 2‚Äì3 minutes.
  4. Spice it Up: Add turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne, salt, and black pepper. Stir to coat the meat and vegetables in the spices.
  5. Add Legumes & Water: Add chickpeas or lentils, then pour in the water or broth. Bring to a boil.
  6. Simmer: Lower the heat and simmer for 45‚Äì60 minutes, or until meat and legumes are tender.
  7. Add Pasta: Add the orzo, vermicelli, or rice during the last 10‚Äì15 minutes of cooking.
  8. Finish: Taste and adjust seasoning. Add chopped parsley or cilantro before serving.

To Serve:

Serve hot with lemon wedges and fresh bread on the side. Squeeze lemon juice into the soup for extra brightness. Enjoy!
