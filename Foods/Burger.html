Smash Burger

Description

A smashed burger is a type of burger made by pressing a ball of ground beef flat onto a hot griddle or skillet, creating a crispy, caramelized crust while keeping the inside juicy. It's usually thin, flavorful, and served with classic toppings like cheese, pickles, onions, and sauce on a soft bun.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 IB Ground Beef (80/20 Lean)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • American Cheese
  • Burger buns (Brioche Buns are the best!)
  • 1/2 Onion sliced or diced
  • 1/2 stick of Butter

    • Optional Toppings

  • pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Season the meat with the salt and pepper; then divide them into 3 oz balls and put them back into the fridge.
  2. Crank the skillet to high. Grab your buns and spread a good amount of butter on those nice buns.
  3. While the skillet is heating, place the buttered side of the buns on the skillet and take them off once golden brown.
  4. Now set those buns aside and bring out that meat, place the meat onto the skillet and JUST SMASH EM with all your might. they should be reallll thin.
  5. After smashing the meats, place the sliced or diced onions ontop of them. After 2 minutes you will flip them.
  6. Now that you flipped them add a slice of American cheese to each patty. Wait two minutes again
  7. Lastly put a patty into a bun, or two if you real hungry. then garnish it however you please such as pickles, tomatoes and lettuce.

BONUS SAUCE

to liven up the burger I reccomend making this sauce. all you need is:

  • Mayonnaise: 2 tbsp
  • Ketchup: 1 tbsp
  • Mustard: 1 tsp
  • Pickle relish: 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder: 1/4 tsp
  • Onion powder: 1/4 tsp
  • Vinegar or lemon juice: A few drops
Just mix all these Ingredients together and you will have the sauce ready to put in the burger.
