Description

Matcha is a finely ground green tea powder made from specially grown and processed tea leaves. Originating from Japan, it's known for its vibrant green color, earthy flavor, and smooth, slightly bitter taste. Rich in antioxidants and L-theanine, matcha provides a calm, focused energy and is traditionally whisked with hot water to make a frothy, refreshing drink.

Instructions:

  1. Sift the Matcha: Use a fine sifter to sift 1 tsp of matcha powder into your bowl. This prevents clumps and gives a smoother drink.
